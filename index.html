Introduction: The Sin of Certainty

Learning Objective: Understand that certainty is a premature termination of computation. In complexity, being "sure" is often a biological shortcut, not an ethical strength.

The brain craves certainty because it is metabolically cheap. Doubting requires energy. However, in chaotic systems, certainty is mathematically equivalent to blindness.
